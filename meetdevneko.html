My story

It has always been my dream to code.

Since I've been in school, it's always been my dream to become an independent code writer. It started when I saw people on YouTube just coding without commenting or people's projects published on GitHub. This freedom to just code what you want with different programming languages is fascinating to me. One thing I found really cool is the community behind sites like Stack Overflow where you have problems and share them with others and try to solve your problem with them. That is why I am here and have dared to take the first step. In 2021 I programmed a Discord bot and then I created my own website which was very outdated and I reworked it in 2023 and again in 2024 haha
